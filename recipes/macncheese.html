Mac and Cheese

Description

Creamy goodness of cheese added to pasta, this dish will make you continually come back for more and more. The deliciousness is unparalleled.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of choice
  • Cheese of choice
  • Butter
  • Milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add pasta and boil until done
  3. Drain pasta
  4. Add butter to pasta and mix until coated
  5. Melt cheese and add to pasta
  6. Add milk if desired to add creamy texture
